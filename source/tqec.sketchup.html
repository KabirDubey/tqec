class SketchUpModel[source]

Bases: object

The class to represent a SketchUp model.

Currently, we only support a single connected component of the model and the model is represented as a directed graph. The nodes of the graph are the cube instances and the edges are the connector instances. The position of the instances can be inferred from the connectivity of the graph.
